FALL 2026

Fall Ball / PLL Sixes

FAST-PACED SIXES · ELITE COMPETITION

PLL Sixes Jam

Experience Olympic-style Sixes lacrosse this fall, powered by PLL Play and presented by Battle Lacrosse Club. Sixes Jam brings together teams from across the region in a high-energy format designed to maximize player development through fast play, more possessions, and nonstop action.

Skills Clinics — Lambert Upper Field, Sundays 2:00–3:30 PM. Clinic dates align with PLL Sixes tournament weekends; attendance is encouraged but not mandatory for registered PLL Sixes players.

League Format

  • Olympic-style Sixes gameplay — fast-paced action with more possessions, touches, and scoring opportunities
  • Championship bracket — each division crowns a champion
  • All-Star Games — coaches vote to select rosters, coached by PLL professionals
  • Additional PLL Pro Clinics planned during Sessions 1 and 3 (subject to final confirmation)

Why Play Sixes?

  • More touches for every player on the field
  • Faster decision-making and improved lacrosse IQ
  • High-repetition game environment
  • Smaller-sided play that develops both offensive and defensive skills
  • Experience the format being played on the international stage

Every registered player receives an official PLL Juniors League Pinnie/Jersey included with registration.
