KINDERGARTEN – 8TH GRADE

Boys Program

Competitive and recreational lacrosse for players in Kindergarten through 8th grade — whether he's picking up a stick for the first time or sharpening skills for high school competition.

LITTLE LAXERS · K–1ST

Little Laxers

Our Little Laxers program is the perfect introduction to lacrosse for Kindergarten and 1st grade boys and girls. This fun, low-pressure skills clinic focuses on developing the fundamentals while building confidence, coordination, and a love for the game.

Players will learn basic stick skills, passing, catching, cradling, ground balls, and movement through age-appropriate games and activities. There are no pads required — just a lacrosse stick, athletic shoes, and a great attitude!

Designed to be an easy, low-commitment way to try lacrosse, especially for families already balancing another sport. Practices are held just once per week on Sunday afternoons.

Equipment
Lacrosse stick Athletic shoes
Register — $100
U9 · FALL 2026 – PLL SIXES

U9

Sunday Clinics: Designed for boys in 2nd through 3rd grade who are new to lacrosse or have playing experience. Whether your child is trying the sport for the first time or looking to build a stronger foundation before the spring season, this program provides a fun, welcoming environment to learn the game.

Players will develop the core fundamentals of lacrosse, including passing, catching, cradling, ground balls, shooting, dodging, defensive positioning, and game concepts. Sessions are fast-paced, encouraging, and designed to help players gain confidence while having fun.

It is a great fit for multi-sport athletes. With just one Sunday afternoon practice each week, it's an easy, low-commitment way to experience lacrosse without interfering with other sports and activities.

PLL - Sixes: Only three Sundays of tournaments in September and October. Take what you learn from our Sunday clinics and compete against other teams in international style play!

MIDDLE SCHOOL · 6TH–8TH

Middle School Select

Stick Skills Clinics: Designed for Middle School boys who are new to lacrosse or have playing experience. Whether your child is trying the sport for the first time or looking to build a stronger foundation before the spring season, this program provides a fun, welcoming environment to learn the game.

Players will develop/fine tune the core fundamentals of lacrosse, including passing, catching, cradling, ground balls, shooting, dodging, defensive positioning, and game concepts. Sessions are fast-paced, encouraging, and designed to help players gain confidence and prepare for the Spring season.

It is a great way for multi-sport athletes to stay engaged with their stick skills, while playing other sports.

Lambert Select Tryout/Evaluation: Lambert Select Tryout/Evaluation for the Spring 2027 season will take place on Sunday, October 25, 2026.

All players must attend the tryout/evaluation to be considered for the Lambert Select teams.

Equipment
Helmet Chest Pad Arm Guards Gloves Mouth Guard Lacrosse stick
Fall Registration — $200
Important: at the final Sunday evening practice in October, a mandatory Select Tryout/Evaluation will be held for all Middle School players. This determines spring team placement and is required attendance for every participant.
