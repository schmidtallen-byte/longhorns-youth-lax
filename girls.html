KINDERGARTEN – 8TH GRADE

Girls Program

The Girls LYLA program is divided by age group, with a path from first sticks all the way to middle school select teams.

U8 · K–2ND

U8

Our U8 program is fall clinic based. This is where most players are introduced to the sport for the first time. Lacrosse can be difficult for the littles, so our goal is to create an encouraging and fun environment! The weekly clinics teach basic skills and make learning the game fun!

We have lots of extra equipment, so if you don't own or have these items, don't worry — we do! The only item required to bring is a mouthguard.

Spring format is game play and practices. More information to come following completion of fall.

Equipment
Goggles Mouthguard Lacrosse stick
Register — $175
U10 · 3RD–4TH

U10

Fall format is weekly clinics, run one time per week. We take the fall off-season to develop and grow the team's skills ahead of spring league play. Sessions include agility training, game scenario breakdowns and scrimmages.

Fall Schedule
September 13 – October 25 Sundays Time: TBD Upper Field turf

The U10 program does league play in the spring, which consists of weekly practices and games. The way teams are split depends on the players registered — but we'll communicate how teams are divided at the beginning of each season.

MIDDLE SCHOOL · 5TH–6TH

Junior Select

Fall format is weekly clinics, run one time per week and include agility training, game simulation breakdowns and scrimmages. We take the fall off-season to assist players transition from short field to full field style play and grow the team's skills ahead of spring.

Spring we are in-season for league play. Information will be available following completion of fall.

Equipment
Goggles Mouthguard Lacrosse stick
Register — $200
MIDDLE SCHOOL · 7TH–8TH

Senior Select

Fall format is weekly clinics, run one time per week and include agility training, game simulation breakdowns and scrimmages. We take the fall off-season to develop and grow the team's skills ahead of spring.

Spring we are in-season for league play consisting of weekly practices and games. Information will be available following completion of fall.

Important: at the final Sunday evening practice in October, we will have all Middle School players partake in a evaluation to determine team placement for Spring.
