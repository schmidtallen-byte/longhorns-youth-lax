#JoinTheHerd

Boys Program Girls Program

Boys and girls, new and experienced players, kindergarten through 8th grade, join us! Our programs are designed to fit every type of player. It's clear youth sports have changed. Kids are pressured into choosing one sport at very early ages. Players who don't start young often end up behind their peers skill wise, lack confidence and are left too intimidated to try a new sport. Parents face the pressure too. All of which ends up making kids and their families think 'play early or not at all.' At LYLA, we aim to grow the sport and increase participation. We support multi-sport participation, believe less can be more at this age, and prioritize enjoyment of the game.

  • New players focus on building confidence and connecting with teammates, without fear of making mistakes.
  • Experienced players get more competitive play, an emphasis on team development over individual success, and advanced skill-building.

Our motto: Attract new players. Grow the program. Success will follow.

Whether you're a kindergartener who already has game, an 8th grader new to lacrosse, or a 4th grader who plays year-round — there's a place for you at LYLA. #JoinTheHerd

Spring Season

Our Spring season is league play, with practices multiple times per week depending on team.

Fall Season

Weekly clinic instruction. As lacrosse is a spring sport, our fall focus is on growth, development, and love for the game.
