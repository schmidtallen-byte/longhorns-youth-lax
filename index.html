Our Mission

Longhorn Youth Lacrosse Association is a youth lacrosse organization dedicated to growing the sport and making youth sports a fun experience for all. Our mission is to help kids find the fun in playing lacrosse at all age levels. New or experienced, kindergarten through 8th grade, we welcome any and all to put a stick in their hands and find out what the sport of lacrosse can do for you!

We offer boys' and girls' programs for Kindergarten through 8th grade.

Spring Season

Our Spring season is league play and includes practices twice per week plus games.

Fall Season

Our Fall season includes weekly clinic instruction and is considered the off-season, since lacrosse is a spring sport. The focus is growth, development, and love for the game.
